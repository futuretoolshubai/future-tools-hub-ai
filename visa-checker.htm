AI Visa Document Checker

Paste your documents or type what you have and get a checklist. (Demo page)

How to use (very easy):
  1. Copy the example message below.
  2. Open ChatGPT or any AI chat and paste it.
  3. Replace details (country, visa type, your docs) and send — AI will reply.
Example:
I am applying for a tourist visa from Pakistan to UAE. My provided documents: Passport (valid 6 months), 2 passport photos, hotel booking.
