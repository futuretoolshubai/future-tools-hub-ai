Health Symptom Checker

This is a general guidance tool. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice. For emergencies call local emergency services.

Example prompt (copy & paste): 
System: You are a health assistant. You are NOT a doctor. Provide likely conditions, simple home remedies, and red flags requiring urgent care.

User: Symptoms: fever 38.5°C, cough, sore throat. Age: 35, Gender: Male, Duration: 2 days.

If breathing difficulty, chest pain, fainting or severe bleeding occur — seek emergency care immediately.
